Outline Label

In need of a label, but not the hefty background colors they bring? Replace the default modifier classes with the .label-outline-* ones to remove all background and colors on any label.

Copy <label class="wsp-label label-outline-primary">Primary</label>
<label class="wsp-label label-outline-secondary">Secondary</label>
<label class="wsp-label label-outline-success">Success</label>
<label class="wsp-label label-outline-danger">Danger</label>
<label class="wsp-label label-outline-warning">Warning</label>
<label class="wsp-label label-outline-info">Info</label>
