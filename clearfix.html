Clearfix

Quickly and easily clear floated content within a container by adding a clearfix utility.

Example

Easily clear floats by adding .clearfix to the parent element.

Copy <div class="wsp-bg-success clearfix">
    <button class="wsp-button button-secondary wsp-float-left"</button>
    <button class="wsp-button button-secondary wsp-float-right"</button>
</div>
