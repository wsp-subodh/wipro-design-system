WSP Design System

Carousel

A slideshow component for cycling through elements—images or slides of text—like a carousel.

Images are Background-images. Filling the container div regardless of the image size.

Behavior you can change:
  • autoPlay: "off"
  • showArrows: "off"
  • hideDots: "on"
  • hoverZoom: "off"
  • titleBarTop: "on"
  • Change slide pauses and fade speeds

Slides only

Copy <!-- Slider 1 -->
<div class="wsp-slider" id="slider1">
<!-- Slides -->
  <div style="background-image:url(images/slider/slide1.svg)"></div>
  <div style="background-image:url(images/slider/slide2.svg)"></div>
  <div style="background-image:url(images/slider/slide3.svg)"></div>
</div>

<!-- <Script> -->
$("#slider1").sliderResponsive({
  fadeSpeed: 300,
  autoPlay: "on",
  hideDots: "on"
});
<!-- </Script> -->
