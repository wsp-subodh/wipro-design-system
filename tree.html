WSP Design System

Tree

A tree view represents a hierarchical view of information, where each item can have a number of subitems..

Example

  • TECH
    • Company Maintenance
    • Employees
      • Reports
        • Report1
        • Report2
        • Report3
      • Employee Maint.
    • Human Resources
  • XRP
    • Company Maintenance
    • Employees
      • Reports
        • Report1
        • Report2
        • Report3
      • Employee Maint.
    • Human Resources
